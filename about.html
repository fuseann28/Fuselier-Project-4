about

Hi, my name is Ann-Marie Fuselier!

Welcome to my portfolio. I found my interest in graphic design during high school digital art classes, where I realized how design can communicate ideas and shape how people feel. Since then, I've grown through hands-on projects and my internship with the LSU Office of Communications and Public Relations.

Early in my freshman year, I dealt with medical challenges and the loss of a close friend. Those experiences pushed me to stay focused and strengthened how I approach my work. Through real-world marketing and digital media projects, I developed a strong interest in visual identity and social media design. I enjoy creating work that feels intentional and visually clear, and I'm working toward my goal of becoming a creative director.
